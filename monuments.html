Main Monuments

  • Blagovestenska Church – A beautiful Serbian Orthodox church built in the 18th century.
  • Fő Tér (Main Square) – The heart of the village, surrounded by colorful buildings and charming cafés.
  • Szentendre Open-Air Ethnographic Museum (Skanzen) – Hungary’s largest open-air museum, showing traditional village life.
