Discover Szentendre

Szentendre is a beautiful riverside town located just 20 kilometers north of Budapest. Known for its vibrant art scene, baroque architecture, cobbled streets, and Serbian heritage, the town has become a beloved destination for both tourists and Hungarians.

Its unique atmosphere combines history, culture, and creativity. Whether you're looking to stroll through art galleries, enjoy traditional Hungarian food, or relax by the Danube, Szentendre offers a memorable experience.
